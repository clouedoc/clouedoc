# schooling

## Schooling: target acquired

### What I want

- having a diploma (makes mom proud)
- making friends who like computers, like me

### What I don't want

- it to work on things which aren't interesting to me
- it to have a highly recognized diploma

## Candidate schools

### [CYTECH](https://cytech.cyu.fr/en)

CY TECH is an engineering school with formal sciences as well as computer science. The main advantage is getting an engineering diploma and acquiring knowledge in traditional sciences, which is a "young person's game" (it's hard to get when you're older).

### [42 school](https://42.fr/en/homepage/)

42 is a practical programming school with a focus on knowledge exchange between students.

I believe that it fits my use case perfectly; thus, I'm going to try it 😄!
