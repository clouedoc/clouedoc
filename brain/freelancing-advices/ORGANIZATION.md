# Organization

Self-organization and time categorization can help you to enjoy a stress-free freelancer life.

## Pieces of advice

### Plan ahead

In other words, use a calendar to organize your time. Once you enter a time zone dedicated to a task, do it for the amount of time you assigned to it. Once you're out, you're out.

### Have a shutdown routine

Once you pick up the task again, you need to know where you left off; otherwise, you might end up spending 10 minutes being lost, not knowing what the next step to take is.

A shutdown routine is "writing down the next steps."

### Keep a log of what you've done

Doing so will help you track what happened and what is still on the TODO list.

### Measure time

Measuring time allows you to measure if you were efficient or not.
